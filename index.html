About

I'm Pedro Neves. I build games and tools in Unity and Unreal, mostly in C#, and I teach Unity here in Portugal.

I get into all of it — story and design, tech art, VFX. I care a lot about how things look. Sometimes I'm in 3D for months, sometimes pixel or drawing; sometimes I'm deep in Shader Graph, or fussing with lights and post until it feels right.

Most days I'm in Adobe, Blender, Unity, Git, and Notion — art and motion, 3D, the engine, version control, and keeping the chaos sort of under control.

This site is the messy middle of a career: jams, coursework, prototypes, and a few Erasmus apps that made it to web and mobile. I like ideas that take a familiar mechanic and bend it into something a bit weird.
